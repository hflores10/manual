Uso de would

Se pronuncia /wud/ y le da a los vervos la terminación "ría".

They`d, I`d , etc

Pron + would + not + verbo + complemento

I would not train tomorrow / I wouldn`t (wurent)

Would + pron + verb + complemento?

Would you travel with me?

Ejemplos:

Juan would be in the school

It would also be (también sería)

Luisa would not eat in her house

Would they run in the park?

I would study english on Saturday

Would you come to my house whtih your wife?

We would not dance in the party
